GRB 余辉理论来源脉络

Section titled “GRB 余辉理论来源脉络”

基础 / review / book

Section titled “基础 / review / book”
  • Bing Zhang, “The Physics of Gamma-Ray Bursts & Relativistic Jets,” arXiv:1410.0679：用于 GRB 基础、相对论变换、fireball model、afterglow framework。
  • Tsvi Piran, “The Physics of Gamma-Ray Bursts,” Reviews of Modern Physics 76, 1143-1210 (2004), arXiv:astro-ph/0405503：用于 fireball、relativistic beaming、arrival time 和 afterglow 基础。
  • P. Mészáros, “The Fireball Model of Gamma-Ray Bursts,” Progress of Theoretical Physics Supplement 143, 33-50 (2001), arXiv:astro-ph/0010176：用于 compactness problem、baryon loading、fireball acceleration / coasting / photosphere 和 early external-shock connection。

标准模型论文

Section titled “标准模型论文”
  • P. Mészáros and M. J. Rees, “Relativistic Fireballs and Their Impact on External Matter: Models for Cosmological Gamma-Ray Bursts,” ApJ 405, 278-284 (1993)：用于 fireball 与 external medium interaction 的经典模型背景。
  • G. B. Rybicki and A. P. Lightman, “Radiative Processes in Astrophysics,” Wiley (1979)：用于 synchrotron radiation 基础公式。
  • R. D. Blandford and C. F. McKee, “Fluid dynamics of relativistic blast waves,” Physics of Fluids 19, 1130-1138 (1976)：用于 relativistic blast-wave self-similar solution 和 BM scaling。
  • R. Sari, T. Piran and R. Narayan, “Spectra and Light Curves of Gamma-Ray Burst Afterglows,” ApJL 497, L17-L20 (1998), arXiv:astro-ph/9712005：用于 synchrotron afterglow spectrum、observer time 和基本 afterglow scaling。
  • J. Granot and R. Sari, “The Shape of Spectral Breaks in Gamma-Ray Burst Afterglows,” ApJ 568, 820-829 (2002), arXiv:astro-ph/0108027：用于 afterglow spectral breaks 的平滑谱形、closure caveat 和 break caveat。
  • R. A. Chevalier and Z.-Y. Li, “Wind Interaction Models for Gamma-Ray Burst Afterglows: The Case for Two Types of Progenitors,” ApJ 536, 195-212 (2000), arXiv:astro-ph/9908272：用于 wind-like circumburst medium 的 afterglow scaling。
  • J. E. Rhoads, “The Dynamics and Light Curves of Beamed Gamma-Ray Burst Afterglows,” ApJ 525, 737-749 (1999), arXiv:astro-ph/9903399：用于 beamed afterglow dynamics 和 jet-break light curve。
  • R. Sari, T. Piran and J. P. Halpern, “Jets in Gamma-Ray Bursts,” ApJL 519, L17-L20 (1999), arXiv:astro-ph/9903339：用于 Γθ_j~1 jet-break condition 和 post-break slope。
  • D. A. Frail et al., “Beaming in Gamma-Ray Bursts: Evidence for a Standard Energy Reservoir,” ApJL 562, L55-L58 (2001), arXiv:astro-ph/0102282：用于 beaming correction 和 standard-energy-reservoir 讨论。

模型扩展

Section titled “模型扩展”
  • M. J. Rees and P. Mészáros, “Refreshed Shocks and Afterglow Longevity in Gamma-Ray Bursts,” ApJL 496, L1-L4 (1998), arXiv:astro-ph/9712252：用于 refreshed shock 和 afterglow longevity 图像。
  • R. Sari and P. Mészáros, “Impulsive and Varying Injection in Gamma-Ray Burst Afterglows,” ApJL 535, L33-L37 (2000), arXiv:astro-ph/0003406：用于 Lorentz-factor stratification 和 varying injection 标度。
  • B. Zhang and P. Mészáros, “Gamma-Ray Burst Afterglow with Continuous Energy Injection: Signature of a Highly Magnetized Millisecond Pulsar,” ApJL 552, L35-L38 (2001), arXiv:astro-ph/0011133：用于 continuous energy injection 和 magnetar-like injection。
  • J. A. Nousek et al. 2006 与 B. Zhang et al. 2006：用于 Swift XRT canonical afterglow、plateau 和 shallow-decay physical interpretations。
  • Peng, Königl & Granot 2005 two-component jet：narrow / wide component、t_dec,w 与 t_jet,n、jet-break masking。
  • Salafia & Ghirlanda 2022 jet-structure review：top-hat / structured / quasi-universal / two-component 喷流谱系、dE/dΩ(θ,t) 和 Γ(θ,t) angular profile、prompt E_iso(θ_v) 积分、afterglow viewing-angle imprint、GW170817/GRB170817A off-axis structured-jet caveat。
  • Berger et al. 2003 GRB 030329 calorimetry：事件级 two-component explosion 解释。

Early afterglow 与介质诊断

Section titled “Early afterglow 与介质诊断”
  • Sari & Piran reverse shock / optical flash papers：用于 reverse shock、shock-crossing time 和 early optical flash 图像。
  • Kobayashi 2000 reverse shock light curve：用于 thin / thick shell、reverse-forward shock 标度和 optical flash 衰减。
  • Zhang, Kobayashi & Mészáros reverse shock magnetization work：用于 magnetization σ 对 reverse shock 的影响。
  • Nakar & Granot density-variation caveat literature：用于 density jump / drop 与 afterglow variability 的非唯一性。
  • Chevalier & Li 2000 wind medium：用于 wind-like circumburst medium、ρ=AR^{-2} 和 massive-star progenitor environment。

SSC、TeV afterglow 与 late-time calorimetry

Section titled “SSC、TeV afterglow 与 late-time calorimetry”
  • Sari & Esin 2001 SSC afterglow：用于 Compton parameter、SSC characteristic frequency 和 IC-corrected cooling。
  • MAGIC Collaboration GRB 190114C papers：用于 TeV afterglow / SSC observational context。
  • LHAASO Collaboration 2023 GRB 221009A：用于 TeV afterglow、narrow-jet interpretation 和 high-energy caveat。
  • Frail et al. late radio calorimetry / beaming work：用于 non-relativistic transition、radio calorimetry 和 true energy estimate。
  • Berger et al. 2003 GRB 030329 calorimetry：用于 two-component explosion 与 late radio energy budget 的事件级案例。

Polarization、VLBI 与拟合规范

Section titled “Polarization、VLBI 与拟合规范”
  • Sari 1999 afterglow polarization work：用于 jet geometry 与 synchrotron polarization 诊断。
  • Granot & Königl polarization literature：用于 magnetic-field geometry、position angle 和 afterglow polarization caveat。
  • Mooley et al. GW170817 / GRB 170817A VLBI papers：用于 off-axis structured jet 的 centroid motion / apparent superluminal motion template。
  • GRB 030329 radio size / VLBI literature：用于 late radio source size、expansion 和 calorimetry diagnostics。
  • Granot & Sari 2002、Zhang 2014、Salafia & Ghirlanda 2022：用于 afterglow fitting workflow、spectral breaks、geometry degeneracy 和报告规范。

事件应用

Section titled “事件应用”
  • GRB 030329：radio calorimetry、5° narrow component、17° wide component、SN 2003dh subtraction caveat。
  • GRB 080319B：Racusin et al. 2008 已 ingest；naked-eye burst、prompt optical/gamma mismatch、narrow core (~0.2°) / wider component (~4°) interpretation、wind-like environment caveat；也作为 reverse shock / prompt optical / early afterglow 区分的事件接口。
  • GRB 221009A：structured jet、TeV afterglow、radio-to-GeV afterglow、model comparison；LHAASO Collaboration 2023 用于 TeV narrow-jet interpretation 与高能余辉 caveat，O’Connor et al. 2023 用于 shallow structured jet，Laskar et al. 2023 用于 radio-to-GeV afterglow / extra component，Frederiks et al. 2023 与 Lesage et al. 2023 用于 prompt fluence / Eiso / energy-band definition caveat。
