# GRB 余辉理论来源脉络

## 基础 / review / book

- Bing Zhang, “The Physics of Gamma-Ray Bursts & Relativistic Jets,” arXiv:1410.0679：用于 GRB 基础、相对论变换、fireball model、afterglow framework。
- Tsvi Piran, “The Physics of Gamma-Ray Bursts,” Reviews of Modern Physics 76, 1143-1210 (2004), arXiv:astro-ph/0405503：用于 fireball、relativistic beaming、arrival time 和 afterglow 基础。
- P. Mészáros, “The Fireball Model of Gamma-Ray Bursts,” Progress of Theoretical Physics Supplement 143, 33-50 (2001), arXiv:astro-ph/0010176：用于 compactness problem、baryon loading、fireball acceleration / coasting / photosphere 和 early external-shock connection。

## 标准模型论文

- P. Mészáros and M. J. Rees, “Relativistic Fireballs and Their Impact on External Matter: Models for Cosmological Gamma-Ray Bursts,” ApJ 405, 278-284 (1993)：用于 fireball 与 external medium interaction 的经典模型背景。
- G. B. Rybicki and A. P. Lightman, “Radiative Processes in Astrophysics,” Wiley (1979)：用于 synchrotron radiation 基础公式。
- R. D. Blandford and C. F. McKee, “Fluid dynamics of relativistic blast waves,” Physics of Fluids 19, 1130-1138 (1976)：用于 relativistic blast-wave self-similar solution 和 BM scaling。
- R. Sari, T. Piran and R. Narayan, “Spectra and Light Curves of Gamma-Ray Burst Afterglows,” ApJL 497, L17-L20 (1998), arXiv:astro-ph/9712005：用于 synchrotron afterglow spectrum、observer time 和基本 afterglow scaling。
- J. Granot and R. Sari, “The Shape of Spectral Breaks in Gamma-Ray Burst Afterglows,” ApJ 568, 820-829 (2002), arXiv:astro-ph/0108027：用于 afterglow spectral breaks 的平滑谱形、closure caveat 和 break caveat。
- R. A. Chevalier and Z.-Y. Li, “Wind Interaction Models for Gamma-Ray Burst Afterglows: The Case for Two Types of Progenitors,” ApJ 536, 195-212 (2000), arXiv:astro-ph/9908272：用于 wind-like circumburst medium 的 afterglow scaling。
- J. E. Rhoads, “The Dynamics and Light Curves of Beamed Gamma-Ray Burst Afterglows,” ApJ 525, 737-749 (1999), arXiv:astro-ph/9903399：用于 beamed afterglow dynamics 和 jet-break light curve。
- R. Sari, T. Piran and J. P. Halpern, “Jets in Gamma-Ray Bursts,” ApJL 519, L17-L20 (1999), arXiv:astro-ph/9903339：用于 `Γθ_j~1` jet-break condition 和 post-break slope。
- D. A. Frail et al., “Beaming in Gamma-Ray Bursts: Evidence for a Standard Energy Reservoir,” ApJL 562, L55-L58 (2001), arXiv:astro-ph/0102282：用于 beaming correction 和 standard-energy-reservoir 讨论。

## 模型扩展

- Peng, Königl & Granot 2005 two-component jet：narrow / wide component、t_dec,w 与 t_jet,n、jet-break masking。
- Salafia & Ghirlanda 2022 jet-structure review：top-hat / structured / quasi-universal / two-component 喷流谱系、dE/dΩ(θ,t) 和 Γ(θ,t) angular profile、prompt / afterglow viewing-angle imprint。
- Berger et al. 2003 GRB 030329 calorimetry：事件级 two-component explosion 解释。

- GRB 080319B：Racusin et al. 2008 已 ingest；naked-eye burst、prompt optical/gamma mismatch、narrow core (~0.2°) / wider component (~4°) interpretation、wind-like environment caveat。
