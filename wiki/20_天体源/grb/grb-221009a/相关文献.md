# GRB 221009A 相关文献

## 已 ingest

### LHAASO TeV afterglow

- LHAASO Collaboration, “A tera-electronvolt afterglow from a narrow jet in an extremely bright gamma-ray burst 221009A,” Science 380, 1390-1396 (2023), arXiv:2306.06372, DOI: 10.1126/science.adg9328。
- 本地 raw：`raw/arxiv/2306.06372/metadata.md`
- 主要用途：TeV afterglow、LHAASO 仪器结果、narrow jet 模型解释边界。

### Fermi-GBM discovery and prompt-to-afterglow analysis

- S. Lesage et al., “Fermi-GBM Discovery of GRB 221009A: An Extraordinarily Bright GRB from Onset to Afterglow,” ApJL, arXiv:2303.14172, DOI: 10.3847/2041-8213/ace5b4。
- 本地 raw：`raw/arxiv/2303.14172/metadata.md`
- 主要用途：GBM trigger time、prompt emission duration、8 keV–40 MeV afterglow、energetics、prompt-to-afterglow transition。

### Konus-Wind / SRG-ART-XC prompt and energetics

- D. Frederiks et al., “Properties of the Extremely Energetic GRB 221009A from Konus-Wind and SRG/ART-XC Observations,” ApJL 949, L7 (2023), arXiv:2302.13383, DOI: 10.3847/2041-8213/acd1eb。
- 本地 raw：`raw/arxiv/2302.13383/metadata.md`
- 主要用途：Konus-Wind fluence、peak flux、Ep、约 600 s prompt phase、超过 25 ks extended decay、SRG/ART-XC light curve shape 约束。

### INTEGRAL/IBIS-PICsIT soft gamma-ray spectral and time evolution

- J. Rodi and P. Ubertini, “Soft Gamma-Ray Spectral and Time Evolution of GRB 221009A: Prompt and Afterglow Emission with INTEGRAL/IBIS-PICsIT,” A&A 677, L3 (2023), arXiv:2303.16943, DOI: 10.1051/0004-6361/202346373。
- 本地 raw：`raw/arxiv/2303.16943/metadata.md`
- 主要用途：PICsIT 200–2600 keV prompt / early afterglow spectral-timing evolution、flux-tracking behavior、T0 + 630 s afterglow dominance、telemetry / pile-up caveat、figure index。

### Radio-to-GeV afterglow

- T. Laskar et al., “The Radio to GeV Afterglow of GRB 221009A,” ApJL, arXiv:2302.04388, DOI: 10.3847/2041-8213/acbfad。
- 本地 raw：`raw/arxiv/2302.04388/metadata.md`
- 主要用途：radio-to-GeV multiwavelength afterglow、radio/mm data constraints、forward-shock model boundary、额外 radio component 问题、multiwavelength light curve / SED figure index。
